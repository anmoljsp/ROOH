    Blood Donation Camp (November 2016)

    The first Blood Donation Camp of the 2016-17 Academic Session was successfully conducted on November 19. The target of over 200 units was achieved in the afternoon itself with a total of 223 units collected during the day. Students, faculty members and external participation saw a busy morning for the Red Cross, Rohtak team with donors choosing early morning to donate.


    The donors were provided with bananas, frootis, glucose biscuits and flavoured milk before and after the donation to help them recuperate fast. Proper rest, precaution and tests were conducted by the Red Cross experts with each donor.
