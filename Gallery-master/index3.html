    Swacch Bharat Abhiyaan Drive

    In support of the Prime Minister of India's pet project Swacch Bharat Abhiyaan, a team of Rooh volunteers and members of cultural clubs of the college conducted a cleaniness and sanitation drive in and around the Brahma Sarovar complex. The event was coordinated by the Hope foundation from Meerut which has undertaken not only the cleaniness but also the beautification of the public places


    We also undertook the beautification of the Brahma Sarovar with the Fine Arts club of NIT Kurukshetra in conjuncture with the team of Hope painting the dirty walls of Brahma Sarovar with exquisite artworks.
