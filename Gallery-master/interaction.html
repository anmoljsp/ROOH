Interaction with the Little Ones

The Rooh volunteers visited the Nivedita Public School to interact with the students and to know their preparations for the upcoming final examinations. The students showed great exuberance and were quite confident about the papers. Individual attention was given to all the students with the evaluation of their past exams, their doubts and confidence in the different subjects.


Parents of the children were all also contacted and coached on how to create a healthy environment so that the students can give their best performance and achieve the school and our's expectations.
