    Science Exhibition

    A science Exhibition was organized by Nivedita Public School on 14 October where students showcased their brilliance with some exceptional utilization of their limited resources to create some exquisite models and projects where they spread knowledge about some various important social and modern topics like rain water harvesting, sanitation and conservation of energy through renewable means.


    The team of rooh volunteers motivated the children for their efforts and suggested how they can continue to improve by learning about modern problems through practical working projects.
