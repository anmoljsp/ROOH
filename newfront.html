Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to provide a high quality education to all those who are unable to exercise that fundamental Right of Education due to financial and social constraints. We strive towards our goal of a Kurukshetra and a India where the RTE is realized in its true form.


VISION: Our vision is to admit the students into a full-fledged public school with modern amenities which is compliant to the needs of current technical scenario so that they can learn, grow, earn and break the vicious cycle of poverty. We also envision them to be active and diverse in extra co-currilar activities and to be able to learn and pursue their true passions.
